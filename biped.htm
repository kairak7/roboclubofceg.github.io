BIPED

Biped is one among the significant projects carried out by the
members of Robotics club of CEG. It involves design and
fabricating a two legged walking robot with 4 DOF. The robot
can be controlled wirelessly from any smart device.
It has Six High torque servos and good quality servo brackets
which forms the firm body. The High current driver circuits
for servos are designed by our members using power
transistors and regulators.It has the capability to be
controlled over any smart device.
An android application,like Blynk is used which makes it easy to be
controlled from our smart phones.
