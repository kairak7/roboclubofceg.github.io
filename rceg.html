ABOUT US!

Robotics club of CEG is a student organization estabilished in 2007
by our alumni which aims to bridge the gap between academic and
practical knowledge, by familiarizing students in the field of robotics
and guiding them on various projects undertaken during their course of study.



The club works towards nurturing the technical minds of our college
by providing a dias for them to showcase their talents. The skills
acquired by the students help them to be industry ready and improve
their prospects of employability.
